Linux image info
----------------

- usage: for running Cosmos Ex device (not for gcc builds)
- boot files on DOS boot partition: with support of RPi B+
- flash_stm32: with support for DS1818 on Hans
- flash_xilinx: with support for XC9572 (slowed down)
- app and first firmware: Cosmos Ex 2.0 (ACSI / SCSI version, november 2015)
